# Welcome to AInTandem

This is the first blog post of the AInTandem project.

## About AInTandem

AInTandem is an open-source project dedicated to realizing a **co-creation space for humans and AI**. We believe that the future of work is not "AI replacing humans", but "humans and AI working in parallel collaboration".

## Blog Topics

In this blog, we will share:

- **Technical Articles**: System architecture, design decisions, implementation details
- **Project Updates**: Development roadmap, version releases, feature progress
- **Community News**: Event announcements, collaboration opportunities, contributor stories
